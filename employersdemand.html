What Employers Look for When Hiring Network Engineers

Comparison of Requirements for Freshers and Experienced Professionals

Category Fresher Requirements Experienced Requirements
Educational Background Bachelor’s degree in any stream , IT, Networking, or related field Bachelor’s degree in any stream, IT, Networking, or related field
Technical Skills
  • Basic understanding of TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, and routing protocols (OSPF, BGP)
  • Knowledge of network topologies and basic hardware components
  • Basic configuration and troubleshooting skills
  • Understanding of essential network services and protocols (VLANs, STP)
  • Basic knowledge of network security principles
  • Exposure to scripting languages (Python, Ansible)
  • Basic knowledge of cloud networking concepts and services (AWS, Azure)
  • Deep proficiency in networking concepts, advanced routing protocols
  • Extensive experience with network hardware (routers, switches, firewalls)
  • Strong understanding of network security principles and practices
  • Expertise in configuring and troubleshooting complex network services and protocols
  • Proficiency in cloud networking concepts, services, and hybrid environments
  • Ability to automate network tasks using scripting languages
  • Knowledge of network management and monitoring tools
Certifications
  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) or CompTIA Network+
  • Additional certifications in cloud or security are a bonus
  • Advanced certifications such as CCNP, CCIE, or Palo Alto PCNSE
  • Additional certifications in cloud networking or security
Soft Skills
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Willingness to work in a team, learn from senior engineers, and seek help when needed
  • Analytical skills and a proactive approach to solving problems
  • Eagerness to learn new technologies and adapt to changing environments
  • Excellent communication skills for interacting with team members and stakeholders
  • Experience working in cross-functional teams and mentoring junior members
  • Ability to adapt to new technologies and continuously improve technical skills
  • Experience managing network-related projects, prioritizing tasks, and managing time effectively
Additional Considerations
  • Any hands-on experience through internships or relevant projects
  • Academic or personal projects related to networking
  • Participation in networking labs or simulation exercises (Packet Tracer, GNS3)
  • Involvement in tech clubs, hackathons, or online communities
  • Contributions to open-source projects
  • Understanding of industry-specific networking requirements (healthcare, finance, telecommunications)
  • Knowledge of regulatory compliance and industry standards (PCI-DSS, HIPAA, GDPR)
  • Extensive experience in designing, implementing, and maintaining network infrastructures
Personal Attributes
  • Demonstrated interest in networking
  • Attention to detail and effective time management
  • Willingness to take on challenges and develop new skills
